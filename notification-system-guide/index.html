üí° Key Concept

Everything goes through a queue. When a business function (like creating a Zoom class) needs to send notifications, it doesn't send them directly. Instead, it inserts records into the notifications_queue table. A background worker periodically processes these records and actually sends the emails/WhatsApp messages.

This approach provides: reliability, decoupling, retry logic, and monitoring.
